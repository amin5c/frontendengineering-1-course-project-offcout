Our Story

OffCourt started in 2019 out of a small garage in Chandigarh. The founder, a sneaker obsessive, was tired of overpriced stores and bad customer service. So he built his own.

We stock only the best — running, casual, and basketball sneakers from brands we trust. Every pair is quality checked before it reaches you.

Today we ship across India and are growing fast. But we've kept the same values: honest prices, real reviews, and fast support.
